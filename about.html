About Fiesta House

Fiesta House is a Filipino Restaurant that serves its customers its finest Filipino foods. Fiesta House focuses on serving customers through its physical restaurant and their website where you can order food through it. Fiesta House as a rookie Filipino Restaurant will provide customers with cultural gentrification. Filipino cuisine is known for its savory taste and appetizing aroma. Ironically, Filipino cuisine draws inspiration from several influences such as Spanish, Japanese, Chinese and Western flavors, where it originated from years of colonization. Filipino cuisine is indeed flavorful, with veggies, meat, seafood and even with their traditional sweet desserts. Its rich cultural past and the richness of flavors is what makes up Filipino cuisine. Filipino dishes are definitely locally-flavored-thoroughly infused with our very own flavors, they leave your taste buds tingling. Fiesta House offers it all! You can taste Filipino Food from its comfort food to its traditional one.


Our Vision


Fiesta House’s primary aim is to be one of the leading quality service and product providers. Our Mission


The company’s mission is to offer a unique comfort and memorable gourmet dining experience through a family oriented and intimate atmosphere. We also work towards providing the best customer and website service to all of our customers, making sure that everybody leaves with healthy stomachs and healthy hearts. It is also the goal of the restaurant to be one of the leading restaurants in the Philippines and to expand further to different locations, as more and more customers are recognizing our restaurant brand.
